GLIMPSES OF THE AMAZON

For critical cartography of the Amazon

It's a video production remixed from the cinematography of the Amazon, including home production, ethnographic documentaries, interviews, video performance, video art, television advertising, and video clips. The project assembles fragments of media production made by Amazonians or for Amazonian audiences. The chapters bring pieces of history composing a narrative that crosses art and technologies in relationship with power and geographic space in the specific part of the region, the delta of the Amazon. The REMIX TEXTURES is a work process produced in collaboration of researching and editing, that was part of immersive meetings realized on 2011-2012 in two urban cities of the delta, Belém, and Santarém. This is a re-edition translated and organized to address foreigners' eyes.
