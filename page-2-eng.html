II_ Intensity VS Power: We are on Strike!



The remains of life revealing the open wounds of the region, a sacrifice committed to the progress of humanity. The cattle route, the flooded forest to ensure the country's development amidst battles fueled by intolerance. We are on strike! Portraits of a past, where the rebel roots are decimated and trafficked as well as children, women and transgenders. Art nudging the imaginary divide between the intensity of the place versus the power that takes over the place: the man sells the weight, sells the name, sells and eats.

III_ Epilogue: between rivers, streets and Amazon streams



As long as there is life, there is hope. The offering makes the point, because in this forest there are sacred terreiro peoples. From the ancestral look, the trail is golden - between tunes and challenges, a rain of memories between the collective and collaborative production of the region. Herzog's dreaded contemplation of the white man in Tupi is seen as a family drama. Untold stories. Between streets, rivers and streams I will be fought, but I will not be defeated. CHAMA Verequete!
